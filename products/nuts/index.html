Cut Anjeer

CUT Anjeer / Fresh Anjeer (Fig) fruit are first dried to form round coin like shapes. Dried Anjeer is one of the premium product sold amongst the range of Dry fruits in the Indian Market. Anjeer now has wide spread usage by the Institutes from Sweets to Ice creams either as a main ingredient or as a toppings.


But due to the size of dried Anjeer it cannot be directly used for the application. Keeping in mind the requirement of the Industry, we have come with cut Anjeer – where each round piece of Anjeer is evenly cut in to minimum 4 small rectangle pieces and then packed as per custom requirements.
