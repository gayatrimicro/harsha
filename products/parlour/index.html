WHEAT CRISPY

Wheat Crispy is in a flake form made from wheat flour. It is extruded, toasted, dried. Sugar, malt, salt and Milk Powder are added for flavor and coloring.


Wheat Crispy is used in hundreds of different food products. It is a very functional ingredient that provides crunch and bulk, and is often used to replace many high cost ingredients such as nuts and grains.

Rice Crispy

Rice Crispy is made from long grain rice flour and wheat flour. It is extruded, toasted, dried. Sugar, malt, salt and milk powder are added for flavor and coloring.


Extruded Rice Crispy is used in hundreds of different food products. It is a very functional ingredient that provides crunch and bulk, and is often used to replace many high cost ingredients such as nuts and grains. The most common uses of crisp rice include: Chocolate bar, nutritional bars, Cakes, Bakery Products, Puddings and much more..


Extruded Rice Crispy has a mellow character. Its Bland flavor, as well as consistent color, shape and size allow formulators to use as much, or as little, as they would like to achieve the desired texture and taste. It adds significant value to any product in the form of increased bulk, flavor and crunch, so that your product will easily maintain its crunch and texture. It is available in various sizes and types of extruded Rice Crispy for just about any type of product.
