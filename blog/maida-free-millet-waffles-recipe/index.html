Maida Free Millet Waffles Recipe

Preparation time: 10 mins


Cooking time: 25 mins


Total time: 40 mins


Serves 3 people


Ingredients


  1. 1 packet of Millet Waffle Premix by Harsha Enterprises
  2. 1 egg
  3. 2 g of sugar
  4. A pinch of salt (to taste)
  5. 0.5 g of baking powder
  6. 0.5 g of baking soda


To make yogurt whipped cream, you will need -


  1. 1 ml of vanilla essence
  2. 1 cup of yogurt
  3. 1 cup of whipped cream
  4. 4 g of sugar


Recipe


  1. First off, separate the egg and the egg white in two bowls. Preheat your oven at 93 degrees Celsius and preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a larger bowl, mix all the dry ingredients - the millet premix, 2 g of sugar, a pinch of salt, the baking soda and the baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the egg yolk and vanilla essence and stir. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Meanwhile, beat the egg whites with a whisk until you get soft peaks. Set aside.
  5. Now, add the wet egg yolk mixture to the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly. Add a bit of water if the mixture is a bit thick. Now fold in the egg whites slowly, until the mixture is fluffy.
  6. Pour the millet waffle batter onto the preheated waffle iron and cook it according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  7. Once the waffle is cooked, set them aside in the preheated oven to keep them warm.
  8. Repeat until all the batter is used up.
  9. Mix all the ingredients to make the yogurt whipped cream.



Remove the waffles from the oven and lay them out in beautiful plates cut up in a perfect triangular shape. Add a dollop of yogurt whipped cream and some mint leaves.


And there you have it, healthy maida free millet waffles with yogurt whipped cream without any hassle!
