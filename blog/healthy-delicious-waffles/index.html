Healthy Delicious Waffles

Waffle is a new kid on the block but is growing very fast as a healthy, tasty and quickly preparable breakfast item to please the taste palates of the millions of Indians. The fast spreading waffle restaurants in the major Indian cities is a proof to this claim and the day is not far when Waffle will take on the other snack items in the race of being popular in Indian homes too.


Sweet or savoury, the waffles are literally being devoured by hundreds of thousands every day. Great for breakfast this anytime munching delights have a variety of types flavors and are best enjoyed fresh and warm from the waffle maker.


Preparing waffle is very simple - It‘s basically a batter of flour, milk, butter and sugar with vanilla essence and a leavening agent like baking soda. Good quality of each ingredient and their ratios are important for perfect taste. Alternatively, one may use waffle premix which is a readymade blend of all ingredients. Premixes for eggless waffles or fully vegan waffles are also popular options.


The batter should be stirred sufficiently (but not too much) in a liquid form and poured into a preheated waffle iron which is made of two metallic engraved plates hinged together to give their characteristic honeycomb shapes. The metal frames with liquid batter then baked for about a minute or two till it reaches its typical golden brown color and crispy texture. The waffle can be consumed plain or with veg stuffing and garnished with delicious toppings choices like butter, choco chips, honey, syrup, chocolate, and so on.


Different varieties of waffle premix

Use of premixes helps make perfect waffles because they are the ready made waffle mix with all ingredients in the right proportions. There are eggless premixes available to prepare crunchy, crispy, lip smacking waffles of different types, such as -



Red Velvet Waffles:

The beautiful bright red color of this waffle when smeared with cream cheese makes a very tempting sight before one greedily takes a bite of it and become delighted with its taste which has a flavour of cocoa. Red velvet waffles are instant hit as a breakfast item with their exotic taste and mouth watering texture.


Charcoal Waffles:

This jet black waffle is making waves with its aesthetics and crispness and incredible savoury taste when served with stuffing of green leaves, roasted tomatoes, mushrooms, garlic and topping of maple syrup or white chocolate drizzle. The premix uses food grade charcoal powder which has positive health effects, from teeth whitening to help detoxing.


Matcha Waffles:

Matcha is the powder form of green tea leaves and therefore gives the preparation its green color and an automatic health advantage - you will be full and energised with one waffle for long time and will gain some antioxidants and vitamins in addition. As far as taste is concerned, matcha waffles have a hint of lemon taste with subtle sweetness which can be further enhanced if served with light, sweet and tangy matcha topping.


Waffle cone:

Waffle cone mix of multiple flavours are available to make ice cream eating more enjoyable. Charcoal waffle cone, Eggless waffle cone premix, Chocolate waffle cone mix are some of the popular waffle cones on regular demand.


And finally the exotic Belgian waffles, one of the most beloved breakfast items from Belgium. But you don’t need to travel to Belgium, you can get the original flavours of Belgian Waffle varieties prepared from readymade mix which is low in cost and maintenance and ideal for take-away or serving.


Belgian Waffle Mix:

Eggless Belgian Waffle mix prepares golden brown, thin walled and crispy Belgian waffles with captivating aroma and taste.


Belgian Waffle Brownies:

This is Brownie in Waffle form, has a rich taste of chocolate with munch of Walnut or Pecans.
