Matcha Waffles Recipe: Vegan & Gluten Free Waffles

Greatly nourishing matcha is the pulverised form of green tea which is all the rave these days for its superior antioxidant and detoxification powers and many other health benefits. Adding matcha to your waffle turns this into a super healthy breakfast, let alone its delicious taste. These bright green waffles are rich in fiber and packed with vitamin C , selenium, chromium, zinc and magnesium. They are an instant provider of energy and are wonderfully light and crispy.

Matcha Waffles

Preparation time: 10 mins


Cooking time: 10 mins


Total time: 20 mins


Serves 4-5 people


Ingredients


  1. Coconut milk ( unsweetened) - 1 cup
  2. Apple cider vinegar - 1 tbsp
  3. Gluten free flour - 2 cups
  4. Sugar - ¼ cup
  5. Shredded coconut ( unsweetened ) - ½ cup
  6. Baking powder - 2 tsp
  7. Matcha green tea powder - 1 tbsp
  8. Kosher salt - 1 tsp
  9. Melted coconut oil - ¼ cup


Alternatively, you may use premix for eggless dark chocolate waffles which is a readymade blend of main ingredients. Add flour, sugar and milk as per the instruction given on the label.


Instructions


  1. Preheat the waffle iron and grease it with coconut oil.
  2. Combine all the dry ingredients in a bowl and keep it aside.
  3. Mix the coconut milk and vinegar together in a separate bowl.
  4. Simply pour content from each bowl into a blender, add the remaining coconut oil to it and process until you have a thick but smooth batter, studded with coconut.
  5. Scoop the batter in the hot waffle iron to fill the cavities and spread evenly and cook for some time till it becomes golden brown. At this stage the waffle becomes crispy.
  6. Serve it hot with toppings of fruits, almonds or coconut yogurt. You may also spread some matcha milk jam for extra green tea flavour and top it with maple syrup.
