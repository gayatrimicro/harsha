Eggless Choco Lava Cake Mix Recipe

A choco lava cake is a melt-in-your-mouth eggless cake with a chocolate centre which oozes out when you cut into it. This can be enjoyed by everyone and it's a favorite dessert for kids.


Here’s how you can make an eggless choco lava cake using Nitin’s Eggless Choco Lava Cake Premix -

Eggless Choco Lava Cake

Preparation time: 15 mins


Cooking time: 25 mins


Total time: 45 mins


Serves 4-5 people


Ingredients


  1. Nitin’s Eggless Choco Lava Cake Premix, 1 Kg
  2. 50 g of refined sugar
  3. 1 g of salt
  4. 6 ml of sunflower oil
  5. 2 ml of vanilla extract
  6. 200 ml of milk and
  7. Cubes of milk chocolate


Recipe


  1. Take 4 ramekins and grease their insides with butter.
  2. Preheat the ramekins in an oven at 350 F (180 C).
  3. In one bowl, 500 g of Nitin’s Eggless Choco Lava Cake Premix and 50 g of sugar.
  4. Add 1 g of salt and mix all the ingredients thoroughly.
  5. In another bowl, add 6 ml of sunflower oil, 2 ml of vanilla extract and 200 ml of milk.
  6. Mix these wet ingredients and add them to the dry ingredients slowly.
  7. Mix all the ingredients to prepare a cake batter.
  8. Add the prepared batter to the greased molds with a piece of chocolate in the centre
  9. Cover the piece of chocolate with cake batter until the ramekin is 3/4 full.
  10. Bake the cakes in the oven for 20 mins. Let them cool for 10 mins once you remove them from the oven.
  11. Use a sharp knife to remove the edges of the cake from the ramekins.
  12. Put the eggless choco lava cakes onto a serving plate.
  13. Put slices of fresh strawberries on top and serve.
